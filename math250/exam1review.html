Math 250 - Exam 1 Review

Example 1

Consider the DE \(\frac{dy}{dx} + xy = x\).

  1. Label the DE.
  2. Use separation of variables to find a general solution.
  3. Use the integrating factor method to find a general solution. Does it agree with your solution from above?
  4. What is the particular solution with \(y(0) = 1\)?
  5. Do you expect Euler's method to do a good job approximating this particular solution? Why or why not?
  6. Do you expect the Runge-Kutta method to do a better job than Euler? Why or why not?
  7. Sketch a slope field for the DE.

Example 2

Suppose a population of rodents satisfies the differential equation \(dP/dt = kP^2\). Initially there are \(P(0) = 2\) rodents, and their number is increasing at the rate of \(dP/dt=1\) rodent per month when there are \(P=10\) rodents. How long will it take for the population to triple?

Example 3


A tour busload of 45 people attend two Florida themeparks on successive days. On Day 1 the entrance fees are $15/adult, $8/child, and $12/senior. On Day 2 the entrance fees are $20/adult, $12/child, and $17/senior. The total charges are $558 for Day 1 and $771 for Day 2.

  1. Identify unknowns and set up equations for how many adults, children and seniors are on the bus.
  2. Write these equations in matrix-vector form, \(A\vec{x} = \vec{b}\).
  3. Calculate the determinant of \(A\).
  4. How many solutions are there to this question?
  5. If possible, and if there is time, attempt to find any solutions.

Example 4

Let \(A, B, C\) be \(n\times n\) matrices such that \(|A|,|B|,|C| \neq 0\). Show that \( (ABC)^{-1} = C^{-1}B^{-1}A^{-1}\).

Example 5

Use Gaussian Elimination to find the inverse of the matrix below. $$\begin{bmatrix}1 & -3 & 0\\-1 & 2 & -1 \\0 & -2 & 2\end{bmatrix}$$

Example 6

Find the solution to the following system of linear equations. $$x_1 - 3x_2 = 1$$ $$-x_1 + 2x_2 - x_3 = 2$$ $$-2x_2 + 2x_3 = 3$$

Example 7

Find the general solution to the following DE. $$\frac{dx}{dt} = \frac{x}{t^2 + 5t + 4} + 4xt\sin(3t)$$
