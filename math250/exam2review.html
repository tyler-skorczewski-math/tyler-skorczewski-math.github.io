Exam 2 Review

  1. If we know \( y(x) = e^{2x} \) is a solution to \(y''' + y' -10 y = 0\) write down the general solution.
  2. What form should you use when using the method of undetermined coefficients to find a particular solution to \(y^{(4)} - 5y'' + 4y = e^x - xe^{2x}\)?
  3. Use the trigonometric identity \(\cos (a \pm b) = \cos(a)\cos(b) \mp \sin(a) \sin(b)\) to find a general solution to \(y'' + y' + y = \sin(x) \sin(3x)\).
  4. Find the amplitude of the steady-state, or long-term behavior, of the solution to \(x'' + 4x' + 4x = 10 \cos(3t)\).
  5. A 2 kg mass is connected to a spring and dashpot. The dashpot provides 8 Netwons of damping force for every 1 m/s of velocity. The spring provides a 16 Newtons of restoring force for every meter of displacement. The spring is stretched 1 meter and released from rest. Set up an IVP governing the displacement of the mass. Classify the system as overdamped, underdamped, or critically damped. Justify your answer.
  6. Solve \(y'' - 4y' + 3y\) with \(y(0) = 7, y'(0) = 11\).
  7. Find a basis for the solution space of the system below. $$x_1 - 2x_2 + 3x_3 = 0$$ $$2x_1 - 3x_2 - 3x_3 = 0$$
  8. True or False. \(\vec{w} = \begin{bmatrix}-7\\7\\11\end{bmatrix}\) is in span\(\{\vec{v}_1, \vec{v}_2, \vec{v}_3\}\). Justify your answer. $$\vec{v}_1 = \begin{bmatrix}1\\2\\1\end{bmatrix}, \vec{v}_2 = \begin{bmatrix}-4\\-1\\2\end{bmatrix},\vec{v}_3 = \begin{bmatrix}-3\\1\\3\end{bmatrix}$$
