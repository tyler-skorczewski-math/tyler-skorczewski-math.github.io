Inside the briefcase you see a cipher wheel and some codes. The codes give you hints for the next sea shells.

To use a cipher wheel you line up the letters on the key. The encoded letters are on the inner circle and the decoded letters are on the outside.

Encoded messages:

  • gfxnq
  • lfx yfsp
  • xyjjwnsl bmjjq


Click "Next" when you've found all three shells.
