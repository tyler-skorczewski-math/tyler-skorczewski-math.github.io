Systems of Inequalities

Basic examples

  1. Determine whether the points below make the inequality \(7x - 3y > 24\) a true statement.
    1. (5,1)
    2. (3,-4)
    3. (2,2)
    4. (4,1)
  2. Graph the solution to the inequalities below.
    1. \(y \geq 0 \)
    2. \(6x + 8y \leq 24\)
    3. \(5x + 3y < 15\)
    4. \(4x + 3y \geq 12\)
    5. \(9x - 2y < 18\)
    6. \(3x > 15\)

Word problem

The American Desk Company makes desks and tables. There are 350 hours of labor available each week in the finishing room. Each table requires 2.5 hours in the findishing room, and each desk requires 4 hours. Graph the number of each that can be produced in one week.

Word problem

Anna is on a diet that requires no more than 1800 calories, no more than 60 grams of fat, and at least 275 grams of carbohydrates each day. She has coupons for a nearby deli for their seafood and crab sandwich and their western egg sandwich so she plans one day's diet using only these two items. The nutrional value of each sandwich in shown in the table below. Write each inequality that expresses the nutrional requirements of the diet.

Calories Fat [g] Carbohydrates [g]
Seafood and crab 410 16 52
Western egg 300 12 36

Word problem

Nikia has up to $6400 for restocking the housewares section at Cox's Department Store. She plans to buy single-serve coffee makers at $139 each, 12-cup programmable coffee makers at $65 each, and 12-speed blenders at $19 each. Write the inequality that expresses the limits on the number of each item she can purchase.

  2. A test is scored by giving 4 points for each correct answer and -1 for each incorrect answer. To obtain an acceptable score, a student must answer at least 60 questions and attain a score of 200 points. Express this information with inequalities.
  3. A plane has two kinds of packages to deliver; each of the first kind weighs 140 pounds and occupies 3 cubic feet of space. Each of the second kind weighs 185 pounds and occupies 5 cubic feet of space. The plane is limited to a total of 6000 pounds of cargo and has 300 cubic feet of cargo space available.   3. $$x + y \le 2$$ $$2x + y \ge 6$$ $$y \ge 0$$

Teach your neighbor

Graph the solution set and find the corners of the following systems.

  1. $$x + y \le 6$$ $$8 \ge 2x + y$$ $$x \ge 0, y \ge 0$$

  2. $$2x + y \ge 50$$ $$x + 2y \ge 40$$ $$x \ge 0, y \ge 0$$

Campus store

The Campus Store orders homecoming mugs and glasses. The mugs cost $1.58 each and the glasses cost $1.07 each. They plan to sell the mugs for $3.29 each and the glasses for $2.45 each. They are limited to $600 for the purchase of the mugs and glasses and need to get at least $1100 in total sales. Identify variables, write this information as inequalities, and graph the solution set.
