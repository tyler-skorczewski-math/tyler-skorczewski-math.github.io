Systems of Inequalities

Translating word problems

  1. To be eligible for a university scholarship, a student must score at least 600 on the SAT verbal test and 600 on the SAT mathematics test and must have a combined verbalâ€“mathematics score of 1325 or more. Write this as a system of inequalities.
  2. A test is scored by giving 4 points for each correct answer and 21 for each incorrect answer. To obtain an acceptable score, a student must answer at least 60 questions and attain a score of 200 points. Express this information with inequalities.
  3. A plane has two kinds of packages to deliver; each of the first kind weighs 140 pounds and occupies 3 cubic feet of space. Each of the second kind weighs 185 pounds and occupies 5 cubic feet of space. The plane is limited to a total of 6000 pounds of cargo and has 300 cubic feet of cargo space available. Write the inequalities that describe the number of each kind of package that can be carried.

Examples

Graph the solution set to the systems of inequalities below.
  1. $$x + y \le 100$$ $$40 \le x + y$$ $$x \ge 0, y \ge 0$$ $$x \le 80, y \le 70$$

  2. $$4x + 3y \ge 12$$ $$x - y \le 0$$

  3. $$x + y \le 2$$ $$2x + y \ge 6$$ $$y \ge 0$$

Teach your neighbor

Graph the solution set and find the corners of the following systems.

  1. $$x + y \le 6$$ $$8 \ge 2x + y$$ $$x \ge 0, y \ge 0$$

  2. $$2x + y \ge 50$$ $$x + 2y \ge 40$$ $$x \ge 0, y \ge 0$$

Campus store

The Campus Store orders homecoming mugs and glasses. The mugs cost $1.58 each and the glasses cost $1.07 each. They plan to sell the mugs for $3.29 each and the glasses for $2.45 each. They are limited to $600 for the purchase of the mugs and glasses and need to get at least $1100 in total sales. Identify variables, write this information as inequalities, and graph the solution set.
