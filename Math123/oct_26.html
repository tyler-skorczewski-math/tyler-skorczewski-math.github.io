Example 1 - Home prices

The National Association of Realtors estimates that the average home price in Omaha, Nebraska was $75,600 in 1990 and $135,200 in 2008. Determine approximately the average annual rate of inflation of home prices in that 18-year period.

Example 2 - Loan choices

A jeweler wants to obtain a $3000 loan for 4 months. She has the choice of a simple interest loan at 10.3% or a discount loan at 10.1%. (The discount rate is a percentage of the maturity value - not the proceeds.) Which one will result in the lower fee for the use of the money?

Example 3 - Investment choices

Example 4 - Selling your car to a friend

Suppose after buying a new car you sell your old car to a friend. You accept a 9-month note (loan) for $3500 at 10% simple interest per year as payment. 2 months later you find that you need cash and sell the note to a third party for $3550. Is this person getting a better deal? By how much is it better or worse in terms of interest rates?
