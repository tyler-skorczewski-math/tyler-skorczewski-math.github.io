Dot and Cross Products

Properties of dot products

  1. \(\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = |\vec{a}||\vec{b}|\cos(\theta) \)
  2. \(\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = \sum_i a_i b_i\)
  3. \(\vec{a} \cdot \vec{a} = \|\vec{a}\|^2\)
  4. \(\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = \vec{b} \cdot \vec{a} \)
  5. \(\vec{a} \cdot ( \vec{b} + \vec{c} ) = \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} + \vec{a} \cdot \vec{c} \)
  6. \( (r\vec{a}) \cdot \vec{b} = \vec{a} \cdot (r \vec{b} )\)
  7. \( \vec{a} \cdot \vec{0} = 0\)

Cross products

  1. \(\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = \begin{bmatrix}a_1 \\ a_2 \\ a_3\end{bmatrix} \times \begin{bmatrix}b_1 \\ b_2 \\ b_3\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}a_2 b_3 - a_3b_2 \\ a_3b_1 - a_1b_3 \\ a_1b_2 - a_2b_1\end{bmatrix} \)
  2. \(\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = |\vec{a}||\vec{b}|\sin(\theta) \hat{n} \)
  3. \(\vec{a} \times \vec{b} \) is orthogonal to both \(\vec{a}\) and \(\vec{b}\).
  4. \(\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = -\vec{b} \times \vec{a} \)
  5. \(r (\vec{a} \times \vec{b}) = (r\vec{a}) \times \vec{b} = \vec{a} \times (r \vec{b})\)
  6. \(\vec{a} \times (\vec{b} + \vec{c}) = \vec{a} \times \vec{b} + \vec{a} \times \vec{c}\)
  7. \((\vec{a} + \vec{b}) \times \vec{c} = \vec{a} \times \vec{c} + \vec{b} \times \vec{c}\)
  8. \(\vec{a} \times \vec{b} \times \vec{c} = \vec{b} (\vec{a}\cdot\vec{c}) - \vec{c}(\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b})\)
  9. \(\vec{a} \cdot (\vec{a}\times\vec{b}) = 0\)

Example 5 - Torque


Consider a 30 cm long wrench stretching horizontally from a nut. A force \(<0,3,-4>\) is applied to the end of the wrench opposite the nut. What is the torque applied?

Example 6 - Track

A 100-meter dash is run on a track heading northeasterly. The wind is blowing out of the south at a speed of 8 km/hr. The rules say that a legal wind speed measured in the direction of the dash must not exceed 5 km/hr if the times can be considered for official records (e.g. WR). Will the race results here be considered for records or will the results be labeled 'wind-aided?'
