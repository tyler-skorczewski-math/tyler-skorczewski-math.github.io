Math 158 Exam 2 Review Fall 2024

  1. Evaluate \(\displaystyle \iint_A y~dA\) where \(A\) is the region bounded by \(x=0,y=0\) and \(x = \cos^{-1}y\).

  2. Find the \(x\) coordinate of the center of mass of the cube \( [0,1] \times [0,1] \times [0,1]\) with density \(\rho(x,y,z) = xy\).

  3. Set up integrals that represent the center of mass of solid \(E\) with density \(\rho = 2\). \(E\) lies under the plane \(z = 1 + x + y\) and above the region in the \(xy\)-plane bounded by the curves \(y = \sqrt{x}, y=0, x=1\).

  4. What are the tranformation equations between rectangular and cylindrical coordinates? What is \(dV\) in cylindrical coordinates?

  5. Sketch and describe the region of integration for \(\displaystyle \int_{0}^{1} \int_{-1}^{1} \int_{0}^{\sqrt{1-z^2}} (2x - 3y + z)~dy~dz~dx\).

  6. Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int_0^1 \int_{e^y}^e \frac{x}{\ln x}~dx~dy\) by reversing the order of integration.

  7. Sketch and describe the region of integration for \(\displaystyle \int_0^{\pi/4} \int_0^{1/\cos \theta} r \sin \theta ~dr~d\theta\).

  8. Evaluate \(\displaystyle \iint_R \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}~dA\) where \(R\) is described by \(4 \leq x^2 + y^2 \leq 9\).

  9. Find the volume of the solid bounded by \(z=0, z = 9-y^2, x=0\), and \(y = 2x-3\).
