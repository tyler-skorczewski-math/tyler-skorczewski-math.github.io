Integral review

Find the integrals for each of the functions below.

  1. \begin{equation*} \int_1^2 x^4 - 3x^2 dx \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} \int_0^x y^2 \sin(3y) dy \end{equation*}
  3. \begin{equation*} \int_{-1}^{1} \sin x dx \end{equation*}
  4. \begin{equation*} \int_1^4 \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}} dx \end{equation*}
  5. \begin{equation*} \int x + x^{-1} dx \end{equation*}
  6. \begin{equation*} \int e^{2t} + e^{-2t} dt \end{equation*}
  7. \begin{equation*} \int \frac{2x+1}{x^2 + 5x + 6} dx \end{equation*}
  8. \begin{equation*} \int_0^{\pi/6} \cos 2x + \sin x dx \end{equation*}
  9. \begin{equation*} \int \cos^2 (2x) dx \end{equation*}
