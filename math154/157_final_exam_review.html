Math 157 Final Exam Review

Example 1

Find the length of the parametric curve given by \(x(t) = 1 - 2\cos t, y(t) = 1 - 2\sin t\) for \(0 \le t \le 4\).

Example 2

Find the fourth-degree Taylor polynomial of \(f(x) = \ln x\) about \(x=1\).

Example 3

Find the area of the region bounded by the graphs of \(y = 1/\sqrt{1-x^2}, y = 0, x=0, x=1\).

Example 4

Find the form for the partial fractions decomposition of \(\frac{8x}{x^3 - 5x^2}\). You do not need to carry out the full decomposition.

Example 5

Evaluate \(\int \frac{dx}{x^2 - 4x} \).

Example 6

A cylindrical oil storage drum of diameter 4 feet and length 8 feet is lying on its side. If the tank is half full of oil that weighs 50 lb/ft\(^3\), set up an integral that finds the force exerted by the oil on one end of the drum.

Example 7

Let \(R\) be the region bounded by the graphs of \(y=x^2 + 1, y=-x+1\), and \(x=1\) with \(x \ge 0\). Find the volume of the solid created by revolving \(R\) about the \(y-\)axis.

Example 8

Evaluate the following integrals:

  1. \(\int x \sin x dx\)
  2. \(\int \tan^{-1} x dx\)
  3. \(\int \frac{\ln t}{t} dt\)

Example 9

Find the volume of the solid created by rotating the area under the curve \(y = (x-1)^2 + 2\) from \(x=-1\) to \(x=2\) about the \(x\)-axis.

Example 10

A tank having the shape of a right-circular cylinder with a radius of 4 feet and a height of 6 feet is filled to the top with water weighing 62.4 lb/ft\(^3\). Find the work required to empty the tank by pumping the water over the top of the tank.

Example 11

  1. Find \(dy/dx\) for the parametric equations \(x=\sqrt{t}, y = \frac{1}{t}\).
  2. Find an equation for the tangent to the parametric curve above when \(t=1\).

Example 12

Determine if the series below converge or diverge.

  1. $$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^3 + 3n + 1}$$
  2. $$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left( 1 - \cos(1/n) \right)$$
  3. $$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n^3}{3^n}$$
  4. $$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n^n}{n!}$$

Example 13

How many terms are required to ensure that using the first \(N\) terms to approximate the series below is within 0.1? $$\sum_{n=2}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n}{\ln(n)}$$

Example 14

Consider a 100 gallon water tank that contains 10 lbs of salt. Fresh water is pumped in at a rate of 10 gallons per minute. The saltwater is perfectly mixed and flows out at a rate of 10 gallons per minute. How much time needs to pass before there is 5 lbs of salt in the tank?

Example 15

Use integration by parts twice to evaluate the integral below. $$\int \sin(\ln x) dx$$

Example 16

Evaluate the integral \(\displaystyle \int_0^{2\pi} \cos(x) \sin(2 x) dx \)
