Final Exam Review Fall 2021

  1. Consider a mountain modeled by\(\displaystyle z = 50e^{-y^2} - x^4 + xy - y^2\) with postive \(x\) pointing east, positive \(y\) pointing north and \(z\) representing elevation. True or False. If you are at point (2,0,34) and heading on a path directly northeast, then you are heading uphill. Justify your answer.

  2. Sketch a contour map of \(f(x,y) = 9 - (x-5)^2 - (y+3)^2\).

  3. Find and classify the critical points, if any, of \(f(x,y) = \frac{1}{3}x^3 + y^2 + 2xy - 6x -3y +4\).

  4. Write the integral \(\displaystyle \int_1^2 \int_0^x (x^2 + y^2)~dy~dx\) in polar coordinates.

  5. Set up integrals in rectangular and cylindrical coordinates that represent the volume of the region below the plane \(z=1\) and inside the paraboloid \(z = 4x^2 + 4y^2\).

  6. True or False. \(\displaystyle \vec{r} = <\sin t, \cos t, e^t >\) is a flowline of the vector field \(\displaystyle \vec{F} = < y, -x, z > \).

  7. Find the equation of the plane through the points P(1,1,1), Q(2,4,3), and R(-1,-2,-1).

  8. Find the equation of the tangent plane to \(z = \ln (2x + y)\) at (-1,3).

  9. Consider the path \(C_1\) parameterized by \(\vec{r}_1(t) = \cos t\hat{x} + \sin t \hat{y}, 0 \leq t \leq 2\pi\), and \(C_2\) parameterized by \(\vec{r}_2(t) = 2\cos t\hat{x} + 2\sin t \hat{y}, 0 \leq t \leq 2\pi\). Let \(\vec{F}\) be any vector field. True or False: \(\displaystyle \int_{C_2} \vec{F}\cdot d\vec{r} = 2 \int_{C_1} \vec{F}\cdot d\vec{r} \).

  10. True or False? The vector field \(\vec{F}\) is defined everywhere in a region \(W\) bounded by a surface \(S\). If \(\nabla \cdot \vec{F} > 0\) at all points of \(W\), then the vector field \(\vec{F}\) points outward at all points of \(S\).

  11. Which of the following vector fields produces the largest flux out of the unit sphere?
    1. \(\displaystyle \vec{F}_1 = (e^z + x^3) \hat{x} + e^x \hat{y} + y^3 \hat{z} \)

    2. \(\displaystyle \vec{F}_2 = (z^2 + \cos y) \hat{x} - y^3 \hat{y} + x^3y^3 \hat{z} \)

    3. \(\displaystyle \vec{F}_3 = z^2 \hat{x} - (x^2 + z^2) \hat{y} + (z^3 + zy^2) \hat{z} \)

    4. \(\displaystyle \vec{F}_4 = (x^4 - y^4) \hat{x} - (z^4 - 2x^3y) \hat{y} + (y^4 - 2x^3z) \hat{z} \)

  12. Let \(D\) be the region bounded by \(y=1, y=x/2, \) and \(x=0\). Show \(\displaystyle \int \int_D x^2e^{xy}~dA = 2\).
