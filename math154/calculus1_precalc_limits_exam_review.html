Calc I Precalculus Exam Review

  1. Caffeine leaves the body at a continuous rate of 17% per hour. How much caffeine is left in the body 8 hours after drinking a cup of coffee containing 100 mg of caffeine? What is the half life of caffeine?

  2. Find the function in the form \( f(x) = ae^{bx} \) that goes through the points (1,2) and (5,8).

  3. Find the function in the form \( g(t) = g_0 \gamma^t \) if we know \(g(20) = 88.2\) and \(g(23) = 91.4\).

  4. True or False. \(f(x) = 32 + \frac{9}{5}x\) and \(g(x) = \frac{5}{9}(x-32)\) are inverse functions of each other.

  5. Complete the definition. A function is ...

  6. What is the doman of \(f(x) = \sqrt{x^2 -x -6}\)? Write your answer in interval notation.

  7. Consider a periodic function that is sine or cosine. We know \(g(0) = 0\) and that the amplitude and period of \(g\) are 5 and \(\pi\),respectively. Write down \(g(x)\).

  8. Sketch \( h(x) = (x-2)(x+7) \). Label any intercepts.

  9. Valley Electric Utility computes the monthly electric bill for residential customers with a linear function of the number of kilowatt-hours (kWh) used. One month a customer used 1170 kWh, and the bill was $100.02. Another month the bill was $120.27 for 1420 kWh used. Find the equation relating kWh and the monthly bill.

  10. True or False. \(\ln 9 + \ln 1 + \ln e = 9\). Justify your answer.

  11. Solve \(\displaystyle \frac{2x}{x-4} - \frac{1}{x+2} = 1\).

  12. Find the following limits:
    1. \(\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{3x-18}{2x-12}\).

    2. \(\displaystyle \lim_{\theta \to \pi} \frac{\sin \theta}{\tan \theta}\).

    3. \(\displaystyle \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{\frac{1}{a+h} - \frac{1}{a}}{h}\).
