Math 154 Exam 3 Review

Example 1

Sketch the graph of the curve \(r \cos \theta = 2 r \sin \theta \).

Example 2

Consider the triangle with vertices at (0,1,2), (4,3,3) and (3,4,5). Which statement below is most accurate?

  1. The triangle is an isosceles triangle.
  2. The triangle is a right triangle.
  3. Both A and B.
  4. Neither A nor B.

Example 3

Show that \(x = a \cos(t) + h\) and \( y = b \sin(t) + k\) are parametric equations for an ellipse with center at \(h,k\) and axes lengths \(2a\) and \(2b\).

Example 4

Find the equation for the tangent to the curve $$ x = \sec t \hspace{1cm} y = \tan t \hspace{1cm} -\pi/2 < t < \pi/2$$ at the point \(t = \pi/4\).

Example 5

Find the equation of a plane that contains the lines \(L_1\) and \(L_2\). $$L_1: x = -1 + 2t, y=2-3t, z=1+t$$ $$L_2: x = 2-t , y = 1-2t, z=5-3t$$

Example 6

Find the speed of the curve \(\vec{r}(t) = \begin{bmatrix}te^{t} \\ \ln(3t) \\ 6t^3\end{bmatrix}\) when \(t = 1\).

Example 7 - area

Find the area of one leaf of the three leaf rose \(r = 2 \cos (3\theta)\).

Example 8

Find the postion vector of an object with acceleration, initial velocity, and initial position given below. $$\vec{a}(t) = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -t \\ 1+t \end{bmatrix}, \vec{v}(0) = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}, \vec{r}(0) = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}$$

Example 9

True or False. The planes \(4x + 5y - 2z = 0\) and \(2x - 2y - z = 10\) are orthogonal.

Example 10

Find the point where the line \(\displaystyle \vec{r}(t) = \begin{bmatrix} 1\\3\\1\end{bmatrix} + t \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix}\) intersects the plane \(2x + 2y - z = 0\).
