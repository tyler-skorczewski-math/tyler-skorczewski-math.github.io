Example - Trucking

A truck driving over a flat interstate at a constant speed of 50 mph gets 4 mile per gallon. Assume fuel costs $1.19 per gallon. The truck loses a tenth of a mile per gallon in fuel efficiency for each mile per hour increase over 50 mph. Drivers are paid $27.50 per hour in wages/benefits. Fixed costs for running the truck are $11.33 per hour. A trip of 300 miles is planned. What speed minimizes operating expenses?
