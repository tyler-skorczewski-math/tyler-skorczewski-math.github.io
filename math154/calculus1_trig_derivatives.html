Calc I Trig Derivatives

Warmup

  1. Draw the graph of \(\sin(x)\) for \(-2\pi \leq x \leq 2\pi\). Sketch the derivative beneath.
  2. Draw the graph of \(\cos(x)\) for \(-2\pi \leq x \leq 2\pi\). Sketch the derivative beneath.
  3. What patterns/observations do you notice from these graphs?

Examples

We know that \(\frac{d}{dx} \sin x = \cos x\) and \(\frac{d}{dx} \cos x = - \sin x\). Use these ideas, properties of trigonometric functions, and other derivative rules to find the following:

  1. \(\frac{d}{dx} \tan x\)
  2. \(\frac{d}{dx} \sec x \)
